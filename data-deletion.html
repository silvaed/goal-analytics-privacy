Goal Analytics - Data Deletion

Goal Analytics does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, address, or account login data.

The app may only store anonymous technical identifiers such as Firebase analytics/session identifiers or notification tokens. These identifiers cannot be linked to a real-world identity by the developer.

Because we do not store personal user accounts or identifiable personal data, there is no personal data available to delete.

If you still wish to contact us regarding data, please email:
footballstatsworld@gmail.com

Please include any relevant device or app information in your request. If we are able to locate any associated anonymous data, we will delete it.
